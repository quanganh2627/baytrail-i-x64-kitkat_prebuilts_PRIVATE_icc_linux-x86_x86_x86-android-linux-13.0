Double-precision Floating-point Vector Intrinsics

The double-precision floating-point intrinsics listed here are designed for the Intel® Pentium® 4 processor with Streaming SIMD Extensions 3 (Intel® SSE3).

The results of each intrinsic operation are placed in the registers R0 and R1.

The prototypes for these intrinsics are in the pmmintrin.h header file.
